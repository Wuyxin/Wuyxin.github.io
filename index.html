   Shirley Wu

Shirley (Ying-Xin) is an undergraduate (fourth year) in School of Data Science of University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Xiangnan He. She will join Stanford CS as a Ph.D. candidate.

Her current research interests include Machine Learning, Graph Neural Networks, Trustworthy AI, Causal Inference. Her goal is to build reliable ML models with a focus on their explainability (to facilitate human understanding), generalizability (to unseen data or domain shifts), and robustness (to data noise), which guarantee their trustworthiness in critical applications.
